# AI Marketing Strategies

Marketing is one of the areas where AI delivers the most immediate and measurable impact. In this lesson, you'll learn how to use AI to develop marketing strategies that drive real business results.

## The AI Marketing Advantage

Traditional marketing requires large teams and budgets. AI levels the playing field, giving small businesses access to the same strategic capabilities as large corporations.

<Callout type="info" title="Market Reality">
Businesses using AI for marketing report an average **30% increase in lead generation** and a **25% reduction in marketing costs**. These aren't just big companies — small and medium businesses see the biggest relative gains.
</Callout>

## Customer Avatar Creation

Before any marketing campaign, you need to understand your customer:

<PromptExample
  prompt="Help me create a detailed customer avatar for my online Khmer cooking class business.

I need:
1. Demographics (age, location, income, education)
2. Psychographics (values, interests, pain points, goals)
3. Online behavior (platforms used, content consumed, buying habits)
4. Objections to buying (and how to overcome each one)
5. Preferred marketing channels to reach them
6. Messaging that would resonate with them

Create 2 different avatars: one for Cambodian locals and one for the international diaspora."
  label="Customer Avatar"
  platform="ChatGPT"
/>

## Marketing Funnel with AI

### Awareness Stage

<PromptExample
  prompt="Create a top-of-funnel content strategy for a Cambodian eco-tourism company. The goal is brand awareness.

Plan 4 weeks of content:
- Week 1: Educational content about Cambodia's natural wonders
- Week 2: Behind-the-scenes of eco-tourism operations
- Week 3: Customer stories and testimonials
- Week 4: Interactive content (polls, quizzes, Q&A)

For each week, provide:
- 3 Facebook posts
- 2 Instagram stories ideas
- 1 blog post topic
- 1 short video concept"
  label="Awareness Content Strategy"
  platform="ChatGPT"
/>

### Consideration Stage

<PromptExample
  prompt="Write a comparison guide to help potential customers choose between our 3 eco-tourism packages:

1. Day Trip ($45) - Includes guided nature walk and lunch
2. Weekend Adventure ($150) - 2 days, camping, meals included
3. Full Immersion ($350) - 5 days, community stay, all inclusive

Write it from the customer's perspective. Help them self-identify which package is right for them. Include a decision matrix table."
  label="Consideration Content"
  platform="ChatGPT"
/>

## Competitive Analysis with AI

<PromptExample
  prompt="I own a digital marketing agency in Phnom Penh. Help me analyze my competitive landscape:

1. What are the typical services offered by digital agencies in Cambodia?
2. What pricing models are common? (retainer, project-based, hourly)
3. What gaps or underserved areas might exist in the market?
4. Suggest 3 unique selling propositions that could differentiate my agency
5. What trends in digital marketing should I capitalize on in Southeast Asia?

Be specific to the Cambodian market where possible."
  label="Competitive Analysis"
  platform="ChatGPT"
/>

- Use AI across the entire marketing funnel: awareness → consideration → conversion
