# AI for Business Productivity

Now that you understand AI basics and prompt engineering, it's time to apply these skills to real business scenarios. This lesson will show you how to use AI to dramatically improve your daily productivity.

## The Productivity Gap

Most professionals spend their time on repetitive tasks:

- Writing emails and reports
- Scheduling and planning
- Data entry and organization
- Research and information gathering
- Content creation

<Callout type="info" title="Did You Know?">
Studies show that AI tools can save professionals **5-10 hours per week** on routine tasks. That's over 250 hours per year — more than 6 full work weeks!
</Callout>

## Automating Repetitive Tasks

### Creating Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

<PromptExample
  prompt="Create a Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) for handling customer complaints on social media for a restaurant business in Cambodia.

Include:
- Step-by-step process
- Response templates for common complaints (slow service, food quality, pricing)
- Escalation criteria
- Response time targets
- Do's and Don'ts

Format as a professional document with clear headings and numbered steps."
  label="SOP Creation"
  platform="ChatGPT"
/>
