# Prompt Engineering Basics

The quality of your AI output depends entirely on the quality of your input. **Prompt engineering** is the skill of crafting effective instructions for AI tools. Master this skill, and you'll get dramatically better results.

## What is Prompt Engineering?

Prompt engineering is the practice of designing and refining input text (prompts) to get the best possible output from AI models. Think of it as learning to communicate effectively with an AI assistant.

<Callout type="important" title="The Golden Rule">
A well-crafted prompt can be the difference between a useless response and a brilliant one. This is the most important skill you'll learn in this course.
</Callout>

## The CRISP Framework

We'll use the **CRISP** framework for writing effective prompts:

| Letter | Stands For | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **C** | Context | Give background information |
| **R** | Role | Tell AI what role to play |
| **I** | Instruction | Be specific about what you want |
| **S** | Style | Define the tone and format |
| **P** | Parameters | Set constraints and limits |

## Framework in Action

### ❌ Bad Prompt

### ✅ Good Prompt (Using CRISP)

## Advanced Techniques

### 1. Chain-of-Thought Prompting

Ask AI to think step by step:

<PromptExample
  prompt="I want to start an online store selling traditional Khmer scarves. Think step by step:
1. What platform should I use?
2. How should I price my products?
3. What marketing channels are best?
4. How do I handle shipping?
Explain your reasoning for each step."
  label="Chain-of-Thought"
  platform="ChatGPT"
/>

### 2. Few-Shot Prompting

Give examples of what you want:

<PromptExample
  prompt="Convert these product descriptions to short, catchy taglines:

Product: Organic honey from Mondulkiri
Tagline: 'Pure sweetness from Cambodia's highlands'

Product: Handwoven silk krama
Tagline: 'Tradition you can wear'

Product: Kampot pepper sauce
Tagline:"
  label="Few-Shot Learning"
  platform="ChatGPT"
/>

### 3. System Prompts

Set up the AI's behavior before your main request:

<PromptExample
  prompt="From now on, you are a Cambodian business consultant with 15 years of experience. You specialize in small businesses and startups. Always give practical, budget-friendly advice. Use examples relevant to the Cambodian market. If I ask something outside your expertise, say so honestly."
  label="System Prompt Setup"
  platform="ChatGPT"
/>

## Prompt Templates

Here are templates you can customize for common tasks:

### Email Template
```
Write a [type] email to [recipient].
Context: [situation]
Tone: [professional/friendly/formal]
Length: [short/medium/long]
Include: [specific elements]
```

### Content Creation Template
```
Create a [content type] about [topic].
Target audience: [who]
Platform: [where it will be published]
Length: [word count or duration]
Style: [tone and voice]
Include: [specific elements to include]
Avoid: [things to avoid]
```

## Key Takeaways

- Prompt engineering is the most important AI skill you can learn
- Use the **CRISP** framework: Context, Role, Instruction, Style, Parameters
- Advanced techniques like chain-of-thought and few-shot prompting dramatically improve results
- Save your best prompts as templates for reuse
