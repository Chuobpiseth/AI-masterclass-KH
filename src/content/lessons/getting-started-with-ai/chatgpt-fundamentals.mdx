# ChatGPT Fundamentals

In this lesson, you'll learn everything you need to know to start using ChatGPT effectively. We'll cover account setup, the interface, and how to have productive conversations with AI.

## What is ChatGPT?

**ChatGPT** is an AI-powered conversational tool built by OpenAI. It can:

- Answer questions on virtually any topic
- Write emails, articles, and social media posts
- Translate between languages (including Khmer!)
- Analyze data and create reports
- Generate creative ideas and brainstorm solutions
- Write and debug code

<Callout type="info" title="Free vs. Paid">
ChatGPT offers a free tier that is incredibly powerful. The paid version (ChatGPT Plus) gives you access to newer models, image generation, and priority access during peak hours.
</Callout>

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Go to [chat.openai.com](https://chat.openai.com)
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Use your Google account or email
4. Complete the verification process

### Step 2: Understanding the Interface

The ChatGPT interface is simple:

- **Chat Input** — Where you type your messages (prompts)
- **Conversation History** — Your past chats on the left sidebar
- **Model Selector** — Choose which AI model to use
- **New Chat** — Start a fresh conversation

## Best Practices

<Callout type="tip" title="Tips for Better Results">

- **Be specific** — The more detail you provide, the better the response
- **Set context** — Tell ChatGPT who you are and what you need
- **Iterate** — If the first response isn't perfect, ask for revisions
- **Use examples** — Show ChatGPT what format you want

</Callout>

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

<Callout type="warning" title="Watch Out!">

- Don't share sensitive personal information (passwords, bank details)
- Don't blindly trust AI outputs — always verify important facts
- Don't use one-word prompts — they give vague results
- Don't forget to proofread AI-generated content before publishing

</Callout>

## Key Takeaways

- ChatGPT is a powerful conversational AI tool anyone can use

- Better prompts lead to better results
